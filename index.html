About us

Back2Bachpan founded in 2019 are serving the vulnerable, marginalized, poor, middle class, depressed and stressed and other people in general which are in dire need of support to mininmize the sufferings.A Group of professional social workers, psychologists, counselors and humanitarians deeply studied these obstacles and sufferings and found this professional organization that can minimize these sufferings. Initially the Organization had focus on psycho-social, physiological, peace building and personality development of the people
